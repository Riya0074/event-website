Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking our services at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large corporate events, and 1-2 months for smaller events. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

Do you provide event planning services outside Bangalore?

Yes, we provide our services throughout India and can also arrange for destination events internationally. Additional travel charges may apply depending on the location.

What is the payment structure for your services?

We typically require a 30% deposit to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due 10 days before the event. For larger events, we can discuss a customized payment schedule that works for you.

Can you customize packages based on my budget?

Absolutely! We understand that every client has different needs and budget constraints. Our team can work with you to create a customized package that meets your requirements while staying within your budget.
